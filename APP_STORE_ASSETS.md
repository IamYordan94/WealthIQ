# Google Play Store Assets Guide

This document outlines the required assets and information for publishing WealthIQ on the Google Play Store.

## Required Assets

### 1. App Icon
- **Size**: 512x512 pixels (PNG, no transparency)
- **Format**: PNG
- **Requirements**:
  - High-quality, professional design
  - Represents the WealthIQ brand (üí∞ money/wealth theme)
  - Works well at small sizes (48x48)
  - No text (icon only)
  - Square format, will be automatically rounded by Play Store

### 2. Feature Graphic
- **Size**: 1024x500 pixels
- **Format**: PNG or JPG
- **Requirements**:
  - Showcases app features
  - Includes app name "WealthIQ"
  - Professional, eye-catching design
  - Represents financial education theme

### 3. Screenshots
- **Minimum**: 2 screenshots per device type
- **Recommended**: 4-8 screenshots
- **Sizes**:
  - Phone: 16:9 or 9:16 aspect ratio (min 320px, max 3840px on longest side)
  - Tablet: 16:9 or 9:16 aspect ratio
- **Requirements**:
  - Show key features: lessons, quizzes, progress tracking, calculator
  - Include text overlays highlighting features
  - Professional, polished appearance
  - Show real app content (not mockups)

## Design Guidelines

### Color Scheme
- Primary: #10b981 (Green)
- Secondary: #a855f7 (Purple)
- Accent: #fbbf24 (Gold/Yellow)

- Maintain brand consistency
